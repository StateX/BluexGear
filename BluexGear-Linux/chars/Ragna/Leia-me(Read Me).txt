Ragna The Bloodedge Beta 2
Compativel com Win Mugen e Mugen 1.0(Compatible with Win Mugen And Mugen 1.0)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Controles(Controls):
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
a = drive attack
b = slash
c = rapid cancel
x = Punch
y = kick
z = Agarrões(Throw)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
especiais(specials)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hell's Fang = Down,Downback,Back,"X"

Hell's Fang Part 2 = Down,Downback,Back, "A"

"It's not Over Yet" = Down,Down, "B"

Death Spike = Down,Downback,Back, "A"

Gauntlet Hades = Down,Downback,Back, "Y"

Gauntlet Hades Part 2 = Down,Downback,Back, "A"

Infernal Divider = Forward,Down,Downforward, "A" ou(or)"B"

Infernal Divider Part 2 = Down,Downforward,Forward, "B"

Ifernal Divider Punch = Down,Downforward,Forward, "B" depois do I.D. Part 2(after I.D. Part 2)

Infernal Divider Axe Kick = Down,Downback,Back, "B"
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Distortion Drives
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Carnage Scisors = Forward,Downforward,Down,Downback,Back,Forward, "A" 

Blood Kain = Down,Downback,Back,Down,Downback,Back, "A"

Devoured By Darkness = Down,Downback,Back,Down,Downback,Back "B"
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Astral Drive
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Black Onslaught = Down,Downforward,Forward,Down,Downforward,Forward, "B"

OBS:Só pode ser executado se sua vida for menor que 50%,e com a barra de especial no máximo.(Only can be done if your life is below 50%,and power gauge is on max)
